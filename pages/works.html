MoonDeal

What is MoonDeal?

Moondeal is a dynamic Chrome extension and website designed to keep online shoppers in the loop about incredible deals. With Moondeal, users can effortlessly track specific products from renowned online retailers like Amazon, Target, eBay, and more. We developed this innovative extension to address the common frustration faced by online shoppers who often miss out on great discounts because they have to repeatedly monitor product prices.

Pantry

What is Pantry?

Pantry is a user-friendly Android application designed with the primary objective of simplifying meal planning. It caters to the needs of groups of individuals, such as families or roommates, who seek a convenient and efficient method to coordinate their weekly meals and share meal information with others.

This innovative app serves as a versatile solution to streamline the often cumbersome process of meal planning within a group. It addresses the challenges of deciding what to eat, making grocery lists, and ensuring everyone's dietary preferences and restrictions are taken into account. Pantry empowers users to collaboratively plan, organize, and communicate about their meals in a straightforward and enjoyable manner.
