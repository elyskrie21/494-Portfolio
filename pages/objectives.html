Career Objectives

As I look toward my professional future, I envision a career that blends creativity, impact, and technical depth. I’m not just interested in writing code—I’m driven to build systems that make real lives easier, more connected, and more equitable. I believe the best engineering comes from understanding people just as much as understanding tools, and I want to be part of solving problems that matter.

My near-term goal is to work on a team that values thoughtful architecture, clean code, and user-first thinking. I want to continue developing full-stack applications and infrastructure with modern tools like AWS, React, and Python—tools I've already grown confident in through academic and internship experiences. I'm particularly interested in scalable cloud services, intelligent interfaces, and applications that integrate machine learning to enhance user experience.

Beyond the tech stack, I’m drawn to environments that emphasize collaboration, mentorship, and innovation. I hope to earn the trust and experience needed to move into a solutions architect or technical lead position where I can influence not just what we build, but how and why we build it.

Long-term, I aim to contribute to impactful platforms—especially in areas like education, sustainable living, and digital equity. Whether it's helping a startup scale or contributing to open-source initiatives, I want to leave behind tools that uplift and empower. I also aspire to mentor younger engineers, helping others navigate this field with the same support and enthusiasm I’ve been fortunate to receive.

At the heart of it all, my objective is simple: to grow into a builder who writes software with purpose, leads with empathy, and never stops learning.
